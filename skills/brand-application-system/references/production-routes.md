# Production Routes

## `template_auto`

Best for:

- social templates
- banners
- simple decks
- repeated size variants
- brand-safe collateral with stable layout logic

Primary tooling:

- Canva-first

## `mockup_semi_auto`

Best for:

- environment previews
- packaging presentation
- contextualized stationery
- image-heavy composites

Primary tooling:

- Photoshop-first

## `manual_vector`

Best for:

- complex packaging lines
- dielines
- signage systems with engineering detail
- illustrator-heavy master art

Primary tooling:

- manual vector production

Do not promise full automation for this route. Produce a clean execution brief instead.
