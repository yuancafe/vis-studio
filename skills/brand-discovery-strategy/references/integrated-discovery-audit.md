# Integrated Discovery Audit

Discovery, audit, and strategy belong together.

## Why they stay together

- Audit without strategy becomes inventory
- Strategy without audit ignores current equity and technical debt
- Discovery without either becomes a vague intake exercise

## Audit dimensions

Review current assets across:

- strategic fit
- recognizability and equity
- formal quality
- system readiness
- technical robustness
- distinctiveness
- application readiness

## Decision outputs per asset

Every asset should end in one of four calls:

- preserve
- refine
- extend
- replace

## Strategy outputs that must emerge from the same thread

- positioning
- audience priority
- differentiation
- brand core
- verbal direction
- design criteria

## Warning signs

- strong symbol, unreadable wordmark
- a logo-only brand with no reusable system
- colors chosen by preference with no role logic
- stakeholder attachment mistaken for real market equity
- audit data collected but not translated into route decisions
