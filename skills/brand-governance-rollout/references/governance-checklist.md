# Governance Checklist

## Ownership

- who approves brand usage?
- who maintains the source files?
- who updates the guideline?

## Source of truth

- DAM or shared library exists
- assets are versioned and organized
- access permissions are clear

## Internal rollout

- team onboarding happens before external launch
- templates are ready for common use cases
- common misuse patterns are explained

## External rollout

- vendors have access to approved assets
- print and digital specifications are available
- transition timing is explicit for refreshes or rebrands

## Ongoing control

- schedule regular brand audits
- log exceptions
- maintain a backlog for phase-two system extensions
