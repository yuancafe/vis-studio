# Playbook Selection Workflow

Use `brand-style-playbook-selector` once the strategic brief is stable.

## Inputs

- category
- audience
- price point
- personality
- key tensions
- signals to seek
- signals to avoid

## Output expectation

Produce:

- top 3 playbooks
- one recommended lead playbook
- fit reasons
- avoid reasons
- warning flags if two playbooks are too similar

Do not treat playbooks as design destiny. Use them to narrow and structure exploration.
