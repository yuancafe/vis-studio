# Territory Workflow

## Step 1. Translate strategy to signals

List:

- what the brand must signal
- what it must avoid signaling
- what level of novelty or restraint is appropriate

## Step 2. Build territories

Each territory should be genuinely distinct, not superficial variation.

Territories should differ in:

- formal attitude
- cultural cues
- emotional posture
- degree of category familiarity

## Step 3. Explore mark logic in black and white

Start with monochrome structure. Test:

- recognizability
- balance
- reduction
- small-size behavior

## Step 4. Stress-test before presentation

Ask:

- can this scale into a larger system?
- is it distinct enough from competitors?
- does it match the audience and price point?

## Step 5. Curate

Present only the strongest set. More options usually weaken decision quality.
