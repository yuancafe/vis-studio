# Review Criteria

Use these criteria at concept review.

## Strategy fit

- Does the direction clearly reflect the brief?
- Can the rationale be defended in business language?

## Distinctiveness

- Is it differentiated from the category?
- Is it more than a trend collage?

## Structural promise

- Does the direction hint at a scalable identity system?
- Can the mark evolve into responsive variants?

## Stakeholder clarity

- Is the recommendation explicit?
- Are tradeoffs named clearly?
- Are stakeholders choosing between directions, not just reacting emotionally to mood?

## Feedback discipline

- Reject comments that only express taste without context
- Ask what business perception the feedback is trying to change
