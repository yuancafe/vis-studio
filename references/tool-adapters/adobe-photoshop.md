# Adobe Photoshop Adapter

Use this adapter for image-driven outputs, mockups, composites, and presentation visuals.

## Priorities

- image treatment
- composition
- lighting and texture cues
- brand-consistent mockup presentation

## Read first

- `BRAND_FOUNDATION_DESIGN.md`
- `APPLICATION_DESIGN.md`

## Emphasize

- imagery posture
- material and texture guidance
- composition and focal hierarchy
- mockup realism and restraint

## Avoid

- using Photoshop for precise vector construction
- inventing off-brand props or styling
- overdecorating presentations beyond the brand's posture
