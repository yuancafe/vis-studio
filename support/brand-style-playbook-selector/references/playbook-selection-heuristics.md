# Playbook Selection Heuristics

Use playbooks to narrow the search space, not to lock the final design.

## Score for fit

Prefer playbooks that match:

- desired perception
- audience expectations
- price point
- business posture
- formal tension

## Score against avoid signals

Downgrade playbooks that over-signal:

- childishness
- nostalgia
- luxury
- aggression
- softness
- technicality

when the brief explicitly rejects those signals.

## Final review

The lead playbook should feel:

- distinct from the category in the right way
- realistic for the client's operating context
- extensible into a full identity and application system
